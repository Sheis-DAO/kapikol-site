Our Mission

Revolutionizing Influencer Marketing

Kapikol is building the future of influencer marketing by enabling micro-influencers to capitalize their influence early through Web3 primitives, creating aligned incentives between creators, fans, and brands.

  • The Problem We Solve

    The Problem We Solve

    Early-stage creators face severe income delays, spending dozens of hours per week creating content with minimal monetization in the first year. Traditional platforms fail to reward early supporters.

    Learn More

  • Our Core Thesis

    Our Core Thesis

    Micro-influencers deliver superior engagement and ROI compared to mega-influencers. Web3 primitives can bring forward monetization timelines and reward early supporters with aligned incentives.

    Learn More

  • Platform Architecture

    Platform Architecture

    Built on Solana for speed and efficiency, our platform enables fan-initiated token launches, staking-based rankings, and AI-powered growth verification with seamless user experience.

    Learn More

  • Tokenomics Model

    Tokenomics Model

    Fixed supply of 10M tokens per KOL with balanced allocation: 30% creator (3-year vesting), 30% supporters (time-based vesting), 40% liquidity ensuring sustainable growth.

    Learn More

  • Go-To-Market Strategy

    Go-To-Market Strategy

    Initial focus on female influencers in Japan's beauty and fashion verticals, leveraging select mega-influencers as anchors while routing growth to micro-influencers.

    Learn More

  • Our Vision

    Our Vision

    Building the future of influencer marketing that is micro-first, data-driven, and decentralized. Where creators are fairly compensated and authentic connections drive real value.
